Morocco became the first AFRICAN country to reach the world-cup semi-final. It is considered the impressive underdog history. They defeated the tiki tak magicians Spain and Ronaldo's Portugal Team to reach the semi-final. It was Canada's penality and France's ballers who passed their net. Japan

Japan are less known in the football world. In the 2022 world cup eventhough they were elminated from round 16, they performed well. They defeated the 2014 champions Germany and 2010 champions spain in the group stage. Seeing Asian country with no football culture defeating European giants who won multiple world cups is impressive. They challenged Croatia in round 16 but the match goes to penalities and they lost.
